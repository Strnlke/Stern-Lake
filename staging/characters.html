THE CHARACTERS

Meet the people of Stern Lake through Throwback Thursday

Every Thursday, I explore the backstories of the people who inhabit Stern Lake. These are their journeys, their struggles, and the moments that shaped who they became before the story begins.

Amanda

Not broken — just braced

Before Stern Lake, Amanda's world was smaller and clearer. Her father was the center of it. When that trust broke, it didn't shatter loudly. It rewrote things quietly. Amanda learned that closeness could turn on you. She became careful, held onto anger because it felt safer than forgiveness. Then Nathan happened.

Mary

Emily's Grandmother

Before Stern Lake became a place of spectacle and silence, there was Mary. To Emily, her Grandma wasn't just the person who raised her after everything came apart. She was steadiness, discipline. Faith lived quietly, not loudly. Mary was a woman shaped by belief long before belief became complicated in Stern Lake.

John

Through Emily's Eyes

Before the mission, John wasn't just an astronaut—he was someone who had overcome doubt from an early age. To Emily, John was the embodiment of perseverance. He believed that discovering life beyond Earth could bring hope to humanity. But as John's commitment to the mission grew, Emily began to see the quiet distance that formed between them.

Emily

A story of love, loss, and strength

Before the stars and the clutter of modern-day Stern Lake, Emily's life was marked by trials and resilience. As a child, her parents, who ran the town's only church, deceived their followers which led to tragedy and scandal. After the loss of her parents and her grandmother, Emily was left to go through life alone.
