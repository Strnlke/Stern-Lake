MY JOURNEY

The ups, downs, and lessons of becoming an author

Writing Stern Lake has been a journey years in the making. Since 2021, I've been committed to finishing this story—my first completed novel. Every Saturday, I share insights into the craft, the struggles, and the victories of this creative path.

I have always loved creating stories, ever since I was a kid writing on a donated Windows 3.1 computer.

I don’t remember most of the stories themselves. I lost them years ago. I just remember writing about friends going on adventures, and how natural it felt to sit there and make something up.

When I got older, I shifted into screenplays. I noticed I started writing about my own life, just dressed up differently. Most of the time, it felt more like a journal entry than anything else.

I didn’t stop telling stories when I became an adult. I just never completed them.

Stern Lake is the first time I noticed a difference.
