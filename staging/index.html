The Setting of Stern Lake

January 28, 2026

In the heart of Ohio lies Stern Lake, a quaint small town with a unique charm. More than just a peaceful getaway, Stern Lake is owned and operated by Stargazers United, welcoming visitors from around the world to learn about space exploration and the mysteries it holds. As we journey deeper into Stern Lake, we'll uncover the secrets hidden within this town, where science and mystery intertwine.

Latest Throwback Thursday

Throwback Thursday #4 – Amanda

January 30, 2026

Before Stern Lake, Amanda's world was smaller and clearer. Her father was the center of it. He was the person she trusted without hesitation. The one she measured herself against. For a long time, he represented stability — the idea that someone could be dependable, present, and worth believing in. When that trust broke, it didn't shatter loudly. It rewrote things quietly. Amanda learned that closeness could turn on you. That the people you love most are capable of doing the most damage without meaning to. She didn't lose her father all at once — she lost the version of him she thought was permanent. After that, Amanda became careful. She held onto anger because it felt safer than forgiveness. Then Nathan happened. This is Amanda before Stern Lake. Not broken — just braced.

Latest: Writing Journey

A Long Path to Stern Lake

February 1, 2026

I have always loved creating stories, ever since I was a kid writing on a donated Windows 3.1 computer. I don't remember most of the stories themselves. I lost them years ago. I just remember writing about friends going on adventures, and how natural it felt to sit there and make something up. When I got older, I shifted into screenplays. I noticed I started writing about my own life, just dressed up differently. Most of the time, it felt more like a journal entry than anything else. I didn't stop telling stories when I became an adult. I just never completed them. Stern Lake is the first time I noticed a difference.
